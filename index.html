About me


Hello! I'm Sonia. I'm naturally inquisitive, and in my professional life I'm dedicated and have always pursued my dreams. I was born in Portugal, and I grew up near Porto, which is where I went to University.

After I graduated as a nurse, I spent four and a half years working in a National Hospital in Lisbon. I loved living in Lisbon, but eventually, drawn by the English language and career opportunities, I decided to move to London to take a role as a Staff Nurse in one of the most renowned Private Hospitals in London.

I fully settled in London, and after a further 8 years working in the healthcare sector, I've leapt into the world of Tech and am now a Web Developer. I'm passionate about new experiences and when experiencing feeling of the unknown, my curiosity gives me courage to keep going!

Hobbies

For me, there's something fascinating about learning languages! I speak Portuguese and English fluently and more recently I've been learning French on the Duolingo app as a hobby. I also find this a very interesting app from a tech perspective.

I love to cycle, enjoy travelling and going to bars and restaurants in London to experience new places. If staying in, I like to relax and unwind by cooking and watching Netflix movies and documentaries.
